How do you wash your hand?

Proper handwashing

  • Wet hands with water.
  • Rub hands palm to palm.
  • Rotational rubbing backwards and forwards with clasped fingers of right hand in left palm and vice versa.
  • Rinse hands with water.
  • Dry hands thproughly with a single use towel.
  • Use towel to turn faucet.
  • Your hands are now safe.

How do you sanitize your vegestables and fruits?

Proper sanitation of fruits and vegetables

  • Prepare 10 liters of water
  • Use 2 Cleenol tablets into the 10 liters of water
  • Soak the fruits and vegetables
  • Contact time 5 minutes
  • After 5 minutes, rinse the fruits and vegetables
  • Wipe with tissue to dry some remaining water
